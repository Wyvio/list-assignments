Welcome to List Assignments!

List Assignments is a helper that takes in a list of assignments, as plain text from a syllabus, and outputs a formatted list
of assignments that can be pasted into Todoist, the widely popular To-Do list app, to be be added as tasks with correct
due date, due time and assignment name.

Formatting the input:
  • On each line, make sure you have the date and time of the assignment due date somewhere on the line.
    • Good formatting: 2020 February 12 Assignment due by 12pm
    • Bad formatting: Assignment due by 12pm
  • Separate the date of the assignment with a tab or multiple spaces
    • Good formatting: 2020 February 12 Assignment due by 12pm
    • Bad formatting: 2020 February 12 Assignment due by 12pm
  • Try copying assignments from the Course Summary portion in Canvas, make these revisions in a text document,
    and copy and paste all of that into List Assignments! They copy and paste the output into Todoist, and confirm.
Note: submit empty input to clear output
